Travelogue: Iraq

Overview

Iraq is home to the world's most ancient civilizations and archaeological wonders. The country offers a unique blend of historical sites, from the ruins of Mesopotamia to Islamic architecture. Iraq has come a long way since the troubling times and is now experiencing a resurgence, welcoming travelers interested in exploring the cradle of civilization.

Must-See Spots

  1. The ancient city of Babylon and its reconstructed ruins
  2. The Great Mosque of Samarra with its iconic spiral minaret
  3. The Iraqi National Museum in Baghdad
  4. The marshlands of southern Iraq
  5. The citadel of Erbil, one of the oldest continuously inhabited places

Visitor Tips

  • Avoid political discussions
  • Hire a local guide
  • Respect local customs
  • Learn a few basic Arabic phrases
